About DART

Detection, Analysis, and Real-Time Tracking of Deceptive Product Reviews in E-Commerce

Project Overview

DART is an AI-powered web system designed to help online shoppers identify fake product reviews before making purchase decisions. With 30% of online reviews estimated to be fake and 82% of consumers encountering fake reviews, DART provides a crucial layer of protection.

How It Works

  • Text Analysis: NLP detects unnatural writing patterns, forced emotions, and AI-generated content
  • Image Verification: AI vision models identify stock photos, AI-generated images, and misleading visuals
  • Behavioral Analytics: Analyzes rating consistency, sentiment mismatches, and suspicious patterns

Why It Matters

Fake reviews influence an estimated $152 billion in global online spending annually. DART helps protect consumers from deceptive practices and supports honest sellers by maintaining review integrity.

Technology

Built with advanced language models and computer vision, DART provides real-time risk scores and clear explanations, helping users make informed purchasing decisions on platforms like Shopee and Lazada.
