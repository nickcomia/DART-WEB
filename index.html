Detection Engine

Three layers of AI intelligence

🔤

NLP Text Analysis

Transformer-based language models detect manipulated sentiment polarity, AI-generated phrasing, keyword stuffing, cosine similarity duplication, and unnatural linguistic uniformity across review text.

📸

AI-Powered Image Analysis

Review screenshots are processed through the Groq API (LLaMA 4 Scout) which performs OCR, extracts structured review data, and evaluates deceptive indicators — works on any platform without scraping or API access.

📊

Behavioral Analytics

Reviewer credibility profiles built from posting frequency, temporal clustering, rating distribution asymmetry, account age, and cross-account behavioral similarities indicative of coordinated manipulation.

🛡️

Unified Risk Scoring

All signals synthesized into a single 0–100 deception score — LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH — with interpretable justifications and prescriptive consumer recommendations.
