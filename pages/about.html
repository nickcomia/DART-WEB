About DART

Detection, Analysis, and Real-Time Tracking of Deceptive Product Reviews — built to help online shoppers make smarter, safer buying decisions.

Why DART Was Built

Fake reviews are a real problem

The rise of e-commerce in the Philippines has made fake product reviews a serious issue. Dishonest sellers post fabricated reviews using AI-generated text and fake photos to deceive shoppers into buying products that do not match what was advertised.

Most shoppers have no way to tell which reviews are real. Existing platforms do little to detect or flag manipulated reviews, leaving buyers to make decisions based on information they cannot trust.

DART was built to change that. It gives everyday shoppers a free, easy-to-use tool that checks reviews using AI — so they can buy with confidence instead of guessing.

What DART Does

3-Layer AI detection system

1
NLP Text Analysis

📝 Reviews text for fake writing patterns

DART reads the review text using AI language models and looks for signs that it was not written by a genuine buyer — fake or forced emotions, templated writing, excessive keyword repetition, unnatural sentence structures, and language that looks AI-generated.

2
Vision AI Photo Analysis

📸 Checks review photos for fake images

Review photos are sent to the Groq API where a vision AI model (LLaMA 4 Scout) checks whether each photo looks genuine. It detects AI-generated images, stock photos, and pictures that do not look like they came from a real buyer — catching fakes that text-only systems completely miss.

3
Behavioral Analytics

📊 Analyzes behavioral signals of deception

DART checks whether the star rating matches the tone of the review text, whether the writing is unusually vague or extreme, and whether the overall feeling of the review seems manufactured or incentivized rather than genuine.

Combined Risk Score

Results from all three layers are combined into a single 0–100 deception score with a clear risk level and a buying recommendation — so you always know what to do.

✅ LOW — Likely Genuine ⚠️ MEDIUM — Be Cautious 🚨 HIGH — Likely Fake
