Analyze a Review

Paste the review text and upload the photos attached to the review. DART checks both for signs of deception.

How to use: Find a product review on any platform (Shopee, Lazada, Amazon, TikTok Shop, etc.). Copy the review text and upload the photos that reviewer attached. DART will analyze the text using NLP and check the photos using Groq AI vision to detect fake or misleading images.
📝 Review Text (required)

Copy and paste the customer review text exactly as it appears on the product page.

📸 Review Photos (optional — up to 5)

Upload the photos that the reviewer attached to their review. The Groq API vision AI (LLaMA 4 Scout) will check each photo for AI-generated images, stock photos, or misleading visuals.
