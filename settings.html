ℹ️ How It Works

When you analyze a review, DART sends the review text to Claude (claude-sonnet-4-20250514) via the Anthropic API. The AI model applies multi-layer analysis:

  • NLP Text Analysis – sentiment manipulation, AI-generated patterns, keyword stuffing
  • Linguistic Uniformity – unnatural repetition and structure detection
  • Behavioral Signals – reviewer profile patterns and contextual red flags
  • Unified Risk Score – 0–100 deception probability with LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH classification

📋 Privacy Notice

Your API key is stored only in localStorage in your browser. Review text you submit is sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com). DART does not store, log, or transmit your data to any third-party service. All analysis logs are stored locally in your browser only.
