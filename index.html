How DART Works

Three layers of AI detection

🔤

NLP Text Analysis

Transformer-based models detect manipulated sentiment, AI-generated phrasing, keyword stuffing, and unnatural language uniformity across review text.

📊

Behavioral Analytics

Reviewer credibility profiles built from posting frequency, temporal clusters, rating patterns, account age, and cross-account behavioral similarities.

🛡️

Unified Risk Scoring

All signals are synthesized into a single deception probability score — Low, Medium, or High — with human-readable justifications and moderation guidance.
