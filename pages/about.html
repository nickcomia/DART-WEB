📌 What is DART?

DART (Deceptive Assessment and Review Tracking) is an AI‑powered system that detects fake product reviews by analyzing both text and images. It combines three detection layers into a single 0–100 deception score, helping consumers make informed purchase decisions.

⚙️ What DART does

  • Text analysis – detects unnatural language, keyword stuffing, AI-generated patterns, and forced emotions.
  • Image analysis – identifies fake, stock, or manipulated images that don’t match a genuine buyer.
  • Behavioral check – compares star rating with review tone to catch inconsistencies.
  • Unified risk score – LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH classification with clear buying advice.
