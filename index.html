About DART

What is DART?

DART (Deceptive Assessment and Review Tracking) is an AI-powered system that detects fake product reviews by analyzing both review text and review photos — giving you a reliable deception score before you buy.

📝

NLP Text Analysis

AI language models check the review text for fake emotions, keyword stuffing, unnatural writing patterns, and AI-generated language that signals a fake review.

📸

Photo Analysis

The Groq API vision AI (LLaMA 4 Scout) checks photos attached to reviews for AI-generated images, stock photos, and misleading visuals — catching fakes text analysis misses.

🛡

Unified Risk Score

Both text and photo results are combined into a single 0–100 deception score rated LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH with clear explanations and a buying recommendation.

🤖

AI-Generated Detection

Identifies photos and text created by AI tools rather than real buyers.

📊

Sentiment Analysis

Detects forced or manipulated emotions in review text patterns.

🖼

Stock Photo Detection

Flags professional or stock photos used instead of real buyer images.

⚡

Real-Time Analysis

Fast results powered by Groq's high-speed LLM inference API.

DART Features

Why DART works better

Most fake review detectors only analyze text. DART goes further by also analyzing the photos attached to reviews using Groq's vision AI — because fake reviewers often use real-sounding text but attach AI-generated or stock photos.
