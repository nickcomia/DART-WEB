Detection Engine

Three layers of AI intelligence

NLP Text Analysis

Transformer-based models detect sentiment manipulation, AI-generated phrasing, keyword stuffing, and unnatural linguistic uniformity across review text.

📊

Behavioral Analytics

Reviewer credibility profiles built from posting frequency, temporal clusters, rating distribution asymmetry, and cross-account behavioral similarities.

All signals synthesized into a single 0–100 deception probability score — LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH — with human-readable justifications and consumer guidance.
